About Overwatch Contenders

Season One continues where Season Zero left off with the top teams of each region battling towards an offline playoffs event and a shot at the USD $100,000 prize pool per region.

Of course, there is more than cash on the line—with organizations looking to fill their rosters for the launch of the Overwatch League, all eyes will be watching this season's competitive showcase.

Broadcast Talent

Chris Puckett
Host
Chris Puckett
Host

Chris Puckett is a host, producer, play-by-play broadcaster, and a former pro player with 15 years of experience in esports. In Overwatch, he is a Winston/Pharah/Zenyatta main.

Alex 'Gillfrost' Gill
Analyst
Alex 'Gillfrost' Gill
Analyst

Alex 'gillfrost' Gill has had a smattering of on-air and production experience in games over the last five years, but it wasn't until Overwatch that he truly dove headfirst into esports. He made his first splash in the Overwatch Carbon Series as both head of broadcasting and host. Now he aims to continue bringing the hype in the next stage of Overwatch esports with Overwatch Contenders.

Josh 'Sideshow' Wilkinson
Analyst
Josh 'Sideshow' Wilkinson
Analyst

Josh ‘Sideshow’ Wilkinson has been analyzing and writing about Overwatch since its release in 2016, providing live color commentary at events such as PIT Championship EU and TaKeOver 2. With a passion for strategic complexity and detailed team histories, the global narratives of the Overwatch scene are his favorite bedtime stories.

Jonathan 'Reinforce' Larsson
Analyst
Jonathan 'Reinforce' Larsson
Analyst

Jonathan ‘Reinforce’ Larsson has competed at the highest level of Overwatch esports on teams like Rogue and Misfits. His competitive insight informed his analyst work for the Overwatch World Cup in 2016 and 2017, in addition to his success as a player on Team Sweden at the recent Overwatch World Cup group stage event in Sydney, Australia. Jonathan now joins the broadcast talent squad as an analyst for Overwatch Contenders Season One. He’s also extremely tall.

Andrew 'ZP' Rush
Caster
Andrew 'ZP' Rush
Caster

Andrew 'ZP' Rush has been broadcasting Overwatch esports since November 2015, watching the game evolve from community cups to premier events like Overwatch Open and the Overwatch World Cup. Whether he's highlighting great plays or explaining high-level strategy, ZP brings a unique blend of energy and experience to the broadcast team.

Alex 'Goldenboy' Mendez
Caster
Alex 'Goldenboy' Mendez
Caster

Since 2011, Alex 'Goldenboy' Mendez has been proud to work in the esports industry. With a resume that spans numerous games and companies, he always strives to create an entertaining and exciting broadcast experience. Hot off of hosting the desk for the Overwatch World Cup Group Stages, he's looking to continue to bring more amazing energy to Overwatch Contenders Season One.

Brennon Hook
Caster
Brennon Hook
Caster

Brennon Hook has been commentating competitive Overwatch since soon after its release in 2016, covering numerous online tournaments including the recent TaKeOver 2 tournament. He enjoys playing Genji poorly as well as watching Team UK dominate in the Overwatch World Cup.

Matt 'Mr X' Morello
Caster
Matt 'Mr X' Morello
Caster

Matt ‘Mr X’ Morello has been an esports commentator for over five years. Starting out as a player, then a coach, and now a broadcaster, Matt provides a unique level of insight to competitive Overwatch from his deep and varied experience.
